### Real User Pain Points (Documented Cases)

#### Business Owner Lawsuit Experiences

**Case 1: $75K Lawsuit Fight**
- Business owner sued for $75,000 for ADA website claim
- Fought for nearly 1 year in court
- **Won the case**, paid $15,000 in legal fees
- Top comment: "Have been sued twice (2 different sites). First was general accessibility issues, missing alt attributes, wrong headings. Second was for a hosted PDF document."

**Case 2: Small Business Birthday Nightmare**
- "I got served the papers on my birthday, so I remember it very clearly"
- Demand: $30,000
- Company: 3 employees, brand not yet profitable
- Spent almost a year battling before settling

**Case 3: UserWay Customer Sued (November 2024)**
- Spent **$1,300/year** on UserWay
- Still received lawsuit claiming non-compliance
- Lawyer advised: "Widget attracts MORE lawsuits as there is software that lawyers use to detect widgets"

#### Blind User Frustrations

- Overlays called **"malware"** by disabled users
- Some users built browser extensions to **block accessiBe**
- Quote: "When I turned AccessiBe off, the site worked fine"
- Screen reader users report overlays **interfere** with their assistive technology
- 85.9% of screen reader users want "better (more accessible) websites"
- Only 34.6% feel web accessibility improved in last year

## 8. Key Messaging Framework

### DO Say (Honest Positioning)

| Message | Rationale |
|---------|-----------|
| "Automated testing detects ~30-35% of issues" | Sets realistic expectations |
| "A starting point for your accessibility journey" | Honest about limitations |
| "Combine with manual audits for full compliance" | Builds trust, upsell opportunity |
| "We test, we don't mask problems" | Differentiates from overlays |
| "No overlay that gets you sued" | Addresses #1 fear |

### DON'T Say (Avoid Competitor Mistakes)

| Avoid | Why |
|-------|-----|
| "Make your website fully ADA compliant" | This got accessiBe fined $1M |
| "Guaranteed protection from lawsuits" | Legal liability, false claim |
| "100% WCAG compliant in minutes" | Technically impossible |
| "AI-powered complete accessibility solution" | Oversells automation |
